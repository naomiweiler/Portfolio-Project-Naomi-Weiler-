Who Am I?

I’m Naomi, a UX designer with a marketing background located in Philadelphia, PA.

Before my career transition into User Experience Design, I developed a high level of problem solving skills while working in customer support as well as excelling in a team environment working on various marketing projects.

I am an INFJ, which ranks as the most empathetic Myers Briggs personality type and is referred to as “the advocate.” I strive to improve the lives of others through design and it is my ongoing passion to build delightful, human-centered experiences that add value to users' lives.
